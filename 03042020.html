    • 03/04/2020 - Design Reconsiderations, Improvements

    Initially, we wanted to keep the railgun compact and have 10cm rails. However, the performance suffers when a short rail is used, so we're going to extend the rails to the full length of our copper tubes (61cm) since we don't have the budget for a very large capacitor bank. A quick recalculation of the final velocity of a projectile (in this case a 1 gram, 1/4" ball bearing) gives a maximum velocity of 82.88m/s.
